### NFR05 – Usability

**Requirement:**  
The system must be understandable and usable by non-technical users, enabling first-time users to view and understand risk information without requiring training or documentation.


**Implementation / Evidence:**  
To improve usability, the interface was simplified and made more intuitive for non-technical users:
- Informational panels were placed throughout the interface to clearly explain key features and data  
- Numerical risk scores were removed and replaced with descriptive labels  


**Design Change:**  
Previously, risk was displayed as a numerical value (e.g. 70/100), which could be unclear to non-technical users.  
This was updated to a text-based system (e.g. "Very High", "High", "Moderate"), making the information easier to understand at a glance.


**Outcome:**  
PASS  


**Evidence:**  
Users can now quickly interpret risk levels without needing additional explanation or prior knowledge.
