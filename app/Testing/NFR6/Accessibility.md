### NFR06 – Accessibility (Text Readability)

**Test Case:**  
Accessibility – Text Readability  

**Requirement:**  
Text must be clear, readable, and meet basic accessibility standards (e.g. appropriate font size and sufficient colour contrast).


**Test Steps:**
1. Open the website  
2. View the parasite risk information panel  
3. Check font size, contrast, and overall readability  


**Expected Result:**  
Text should be clear, readable, and easy to distinguish from the background.


**Actual Result (Initial):**  
Some text appeared too small and contrast was not sufficient, making readability difficult.


**Fix Applied:**  
Font sizes were increased and colour contrast was improved to enhance readability and meet accessibility standards.


**Actual Result (After Fix):**  
All text is now clear, readable, and easy to distinguish from the background.


**Outcome:**  
PASS (after fix)  


**Evidence:**  
Visual inspection of the updated interface confirms improved readability and accessibility.
